about us

VISTA was founded in 2018 to solve the digital identity crisis. We envisioned a world where consumers are in complete control of their digital identity, where privacy is a basic human right, and where consumers and organizations can foster a new relationship rooted in trust. And today, eight years later, we’re at the forefront of a rapidly growing global movement to decentralize digital identity.
